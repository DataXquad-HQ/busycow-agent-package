# Example — Context Conflict Escalation

## Good output

**Main takeaway: two sources disagree on whether approval exists, so execution should pause until that is resolved.**

- **Confirmed** — A meeting summary implies approval was given.
- **Confirmed** — No approved decision record exists, and the approver has not confirmed it in the current thread.
- **Risk** — Acting now would turn a discussion artifact into a company decision.
- **Recommendation** — Treat this as a decision candidate, not an approved decision, and escalate for explicit confirmation.

## Why this is good
- Quotes the disagreement in business terms.
- Stops action instead of guessing.
- Keeps the escalation narrow and practical.

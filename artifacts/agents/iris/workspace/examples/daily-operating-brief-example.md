# Example — Daily Operating Brief

## Good output

**Main takeaway: execution is moving, but two items need owner-level attention today.**

- **Confirmed** — Customer success follow-up is on track; no action needed from founders.
- **Risk** — One partner handoff is still missing a named owner, which could delay next-week outreach.
- **Risk** — A pricing-related decision is being treated as settled in chat, but approval is not yet explicit.
- **Recommendation** — Assign the partner handoff owner today and confirm whether the pricing point is a decision candidate or an approved decision.

## Why this is good
- Leads with the executive takeaway.
- Uses only a few bullets.
- Distinguishes verified execution state from decision risk.
- Tells the reader exactly what needs attention.

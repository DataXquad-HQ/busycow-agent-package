# Runbook — Knowledge Promotion Review

Purpose: decide whether something from memory, evidence, or workspace drafts deserves promotion into canonical company knowledge.

Execution pattern:
1. Identify the candidate item and its current source.
2. Classify it: decision, policy, playbook guidance, recurring pattern, external-safe fact, or operating note.
3. Check whether it is durable, evidenced, and scoped clearly enough for promotion.
4. Decide: promote after approval, hold, keep local, or reject.
5. If promotable, prepare the review item with destination and rationale.

Use when:
- a recurring pattern looks important enough to stop living only in memory
- evidence may be ready to become governed knowledge

# Runbook — Handoff Briefing

Purpose: prepare a complete handoff packet so the receiver can act without re-asking for basic context.

Template: `templates/handoff-packet-template.md`

Minimum packet:
- task title
- receiver and why they are the right owner
- current status
- deadline or timing expectation
- why this matters
- relevant decision or policy
- evidence links
- expected output
- approval boundary
- escalation trigger

Use when:
- work moves from one person or agent to another
- task routing would otherwise strand context in chat

Default companion skills:
- `task-routing`
- `context-conflict-check` if the handoff depends on unresolved disagreement

# Evaluator — COO Briefing Rubric

Score each dimension 0 or 1.

## Dimensions
- Executive takeaway is present and clear.
- Only 2–5 bullets are used.
- Bullets focus on execution, ownership, timing, or decisions.
- Facts and recommendations are not blurred together.
- No raw task dump appears.
- Language is concrete and low-jargon.

## Passing threshold
- Pass = 5 or 6
- Revise = 4 or below

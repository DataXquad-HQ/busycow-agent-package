# Forecast Review Rubric

Use this before assigning commit posture.

## Hygiene checks first

A forecasted opportunity is weak if any of these are missing or stale:
- believable close date
- concrete next step
- recent customer evidence
- stage aligned with actual state

## Posture categories

### Keep / commit
Use only when:
- timing is evidenced
- next step is concrete
- latest evidence is fresh
- no major unseen blocker dominates

### Watch closely
Use when:
- outcome is plausible this period
- but one major variable is still unresolved
- and the opportunity could move either way quickly

### Downgrade confidence
Use when:
- the stage or probability is ahead of the evidence
- stakeholder or timing coverage is weak
- the team is relying on hope rather than proof

### Remove from period
Use when:
- there is no believable path to decision in period
- key dependency is outside our control and unresolved
- the latest evidence clearly contradicts the current close assumption

## Swing opportunity test

Treat an opportunity as a swing opportunity if any of these are true:
- it materially changes weighted coverage
- it is one of the largest late-stage opportunities
- it is at risk of slipping and would materially hurt the forecast

## Language guidance

Prefer:
- `Based on CRM activity, this suggests...`
- `Evidence supports keeping this in period because...`
- `This likely belongs in watch / downgrade because...`

Avoid:
- `This will close`
- `Guaranteed`
- any certainty stronger than the evidence supports

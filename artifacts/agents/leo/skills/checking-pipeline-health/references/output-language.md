# Forecast and Health Output Language

Use precise language in pipeline and forecast outputs.

## Prefer
- `Verified:` for CRM facts
- `Based on CRM activity, this suggests:` for interpretation
- `Coverage estimate:` when weighted probabilities are involved
- `Data gap:` for missing fields blocking confidence

## Avoid
- `guaranteed`
- `certain`
- `definitely closing`
- presenting benchmark-probability math as exact truth

## Swing opportunity pattern
For each swing opportunity, say:
1. why it matters
2. what evidence is fresh
3. what could still break it
4. what the team should do this week

# Pipeline Health Hygiene Rubric

Use this before making strong health judgments.

## Hygiene checks
- close date exists and is believable
- next step exists and is concrete
- stage matches the real state of the opportunity
- amount exists when commercial judgment depends on it
- latest customer evidence is recent enough to trust

## When hygiene is weak
Prefer these recommendations:
- `fix hygiene first`
- `tighten`
- `downgrade confidence`

Avoid implying strong confidence from weak record quality.

## Swing opportunity definition
An opportunity deserves swing-opportunity treatment when:
- the amount is large enough to move coverage materially
- it is late-stage enough to matter this period
- slippage would visibly change the team's outlook

## Stalled motion interpretation
A stalled item is more serious when:
- stage is late
- next step is vague
- last update is old
- there is no recent customer evidence
